The median salary for a mid-level Data Scientist is $128,750 while web developers made a median salary of $67,990. Does that mean it's better to become a data scientist than a web developer? Become a data scientist today: https://engineeredtruth.com/DataCamp

Though data scientists do get paid more, they require a much higher level of education and most have a master's degree (88%). It's a lot easier to get a web developer job especially if you're looking for remote positions or to work for a smaller company.

Also, web development is more tangible making it an easier subject to learn.
